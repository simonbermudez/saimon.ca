About the Lab

Welcome to saimon, the AI/ML subsidiary of Simon Bermudez Enterprises Inc. Our mission is to push the boundaries of artificial intelligence and machine learning through cutting-edge research and experimentation.

Powered by a robust cluster of NVIDIA Tesla P40 GPUs, we run self-hosted open-source large language models and conduct advanced AI experiments. Our homelab serves as a playground for exploring the latest developments in neural networks, deep learning, and generative AI.

From natural language processing to computer vision, we're committed to advancing the field while maintaining full control over our infrastructure and data privacy.

Lab Specifications
